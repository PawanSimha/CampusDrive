Knowledge Base

Everything You Need to Know

Is CampusDrive really free for students? expand_more
Yes, CampusDrive is 100% free for students. Our mission is to bridge the academic gap by providing a platform where knowledge flows freely. No hidden fees, no credit cards required.
How do I verify the quality of a resource? expand_more
We use a community-driven rating system. Every document is rated by your peers and reviewed by platform-verified teachers to ensure you only study the best material.
Can I create my own private study group? expand_more
Absolutely. You can create private "Study Circles" and invite your friends using a unique access code. This perfect for collaborating on projects or preparing for specific exams together.
Who can upload resources to the platform? expand_more
Any registered student or educator can upload study materials. We encourage a culture of sharing where everyone contributes to the collective knowledge base.
What types of files can I share? expand_more
You can upload a variety of academic resources including PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, Excel spreadsheets, and images (notes, past papers, etc.).
How does the platform handle inappropriate content? expand_more
We strictly prohibit the upload of copyrighted materials without permission and any inappropriate content. Our admin team monitors submissions, and users can report files that violate our community guidelines.
Can I access CampusDrive on my mobile device? expand_more
Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and designed to work seamlessly across desktops, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring you have access to your study materials anytime, anywhere.
Are there any premium features or paid plans? expand_more
Currently, all core features of CampusDrive are entirely free. We may introduce optional premium features for advanced analytics or verified expert tutoring in the future, but the basic resource sharing will always remain free.
