# CampusConnect: User Flow & Roles Guide

This document outlines the workflows and capabilities for the three primary user roles in the **CampusConnect** platform.

---

## 🔑 1. Administrator (The Overseer)
The Admin ensures the platform remains clean, secure, and populated with high-quality users and content.

### Primary Workflow:
1.  **User Management**: Monitor the registration queue. Approve or Reject new accounts (Teachers/Students) to maintain institutional integrity.
2.  **Content Moderation**: Review all uploaded resources across the "Social" feed. Delete any inappropriate or low-quality materials.
3.  **Platform Monitoring**: Access the **Admin Dashboard** to view real-time activity logs, teacher performance stats (uploads/groups), and total system metrics.
4.  **Support**: Monitor and respond to inquiries via the **Contact Messages** section.

---

## 🎓 2. Teacher (The Content Creator & Mentor)
Teachers are the primary source of verified academic content and the leaders of study communities.

### Primary Workflow:
1.  **Resource Hub**: Upload question banks, notes, and study materials to the global "Social" feed. Set privacy to **Public** for wide reach or **Private** for personal storage.
2.  **Leading Study Circles**: 
    - Create specialized **Study Circles** (Groups) for specific subjects or batches.
    - Generate and share **Invite Codes** with students.
3.  **Community Engagement**: 
    - Post high-priority **Announcements** within Study Circles.
    - Chat with students to provide guidance and answer queries.
    - Share specific resources or upload files directly into the Circle's private library.
    - Monitor their own activity and engagement metrics.

---

## 📖 3. Student (The Learner & Collaborator)
Students use the platform to access verified materials and collaborate with peers and mentors.

### Primary Workflow:
1.  **Discovery**: Use the **Social** feed or **Subjects** view to search and filter for resources by subject, semester, or resource type.
2.  **Resource Interaction**:
    - **Download** verified materials for offline study.
    - **Mark Favorites** to build a personalized library for quick access.
    - **Rate & Review** resources to help peers find the best materials.
3.  **Collaborative Learning**:
    - **Join Study Circles** using names or invite codes provided by teachers.
    - Participate in Circle discussions via the **Chat** interface.
    - Access exclusive resources shared by teachers within the Circle.
    - Stay updated via the **Announcements** feed.
4.  **Profile Management**: Track their own downloads, favorites, and uploaded (private) materials.
